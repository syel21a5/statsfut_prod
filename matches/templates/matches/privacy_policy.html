Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to StatsFut. Your privacy is of extreme importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when using our website (statsfut.com).

2. Information Collection

We do not require registration to view public statistics. However, we collect basic information automatically through log files and cookies to improve your experience, such as:

  • IP Address
  • Browser type
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site

3. Cookies and Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use cookies to provide personalized content and ads. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (such as the DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to our and other sites on the internet.

Users may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

4. Use of Information

The information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Technical maintenance and improvement of the site.
  • Traffic analysis (Google Analytics).
  • Ad personalization via advertising networks.

5. Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect information processed on our site against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official channels.
